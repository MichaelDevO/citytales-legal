Account Deletion

Effective date: June 24, 2026

This page explains how City Tales users can request deletion of their account data and related app data.

App

App name: City Tales

Android package name: com.michaldev.citytales

iOS bundle identifier: com.citytales.app

Firebase project: citytales-b0e9d

How To Request Deletion

To request deletion of your City Tales account data, send an email to:

citytales.support@gmail.com

Please include:

  • the email address used to sign in to City Tales,
  • a short request such as "Please delete my City Tales account data".

What Will Be Deleted

After a valid deletion request, City Tales will delete app data linked to the account, including:

  • City Tales user profile data stored by the app,
  • route progress, completed points, awarded points, and route status,
  • account-linked app records stored in Firebase/Firestore.

What May Be Retained

Some technical, diagnostic, security, analytics, or legal records may be retained for a limited period where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or service reliability.

Data processed by Google Sign-In, Firebase, Google Play, Apple, or other platform providers may also be subject to those providers' own account and retention settings.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are normally processed within 30 days after the request can be matched to a City Tales account.

Google Account Access

City Tales uses Google Sign-In on Android. Deleting City Tales app data does not delete your Google account. If you want to review or revoke app access from your Google account, use your Google account security settings.

Privacy Policy

More information about data used by City Tales is available in the City Tales Privacy Policy.
